What is PeerConnect?

PeerConnect is a student-led platform designed to support teenagers who feel isolated, rejected, or unheard. By pairing students with peers who share similar experiences, we aim to build confidence, belonging, and a stronger sense of identity.

How It Works

  1. Fill out a short sign-up form.
  2. Browse suggested peer profiles.
  3. Choose someone to connect with and start chatting.
